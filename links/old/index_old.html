What Is This Page?
I've always wished people who knew things shared what they knew so that knowing how to start learning them would be a bit easier. Part of that is sharing resources. Here's my attempt at sharing some.

This should be considered forever incomplete and in progress. If you have some, please email them to me to help make this more complete!
